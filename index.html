Sum By Factors

I wanted to try a 4 kyu Kata for a challenge and found this one called Sum By Factors.
The goal was, given an array of integers, to produce a sorted array of all prime factors and the sum of their multiples, in ascending order.

Various 7+kyu's invloving arrays

Plants and Zombies

After completing Sum By Factors I've gone for another challenge and upped the difficulty to 3kyu this time. This Kata is a fun Sim-like challenge based on the PlantsVsZombies game. You have to calculate how many moves any given situation will last before the game ends.
